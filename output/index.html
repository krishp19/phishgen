1. Introduction
The Silver Oak University is committed to providing clear and transparent guidelines regarding refunds for tuition and other fees. This policy outlines the eligibility, process, and conditions for refunds.
2. Eligibility for Refunds
Refunds may be granted under the following circumstances:
  • Withdrawal from a course or program within the approved timeline.
  • Course cancellation by the university.
  • Overpayment of fees
  • Exceptional circumstances, such as medical emergencies, subject to university approval.
  • The payment was made in error (e.g., duplicate transaction).
  • There was a technical issue that prevented access to the product/service.
  • The request is made within the specified refund period.
3. Refund Schedule
Refunds for tuition fees are processed based on the following schedule:
  • Before the start of the semester: 100% refund, minus administrative fees
  • After the commencement of the semester: No refund
Other fees, such as registration, examination, and laboratory fees, may be non-refundable unless explicitly stated.
4. Refund Process
  • Students must submit a formal refund request through the universitys designated office as per universitys process flow.
  • Supporting documentation may be required depending on the reason for the refund request.
  • Refunds will be processed within 07 working days from the date of approval
  • Refunds will be issued through the original mode of payment unless otherwise specified.
5. Exceptional Circumstance
  • Refund requests due to medical emergencies, bereavement, or other significant hardships will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Students must provide relevant documentation to support their claims.
6. Non-Refundable Fees
  • Application and admission fees.
  • Late payment penalties.
  • Fees for services already rendered (e.g., hostel, transportation, library access)
7. Amendments to Policy
  • The University reserves the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be updated on the university website.
